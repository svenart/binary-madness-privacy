1. General Information

“Binary Madness” is an offline puzzle game. Protecting your personal data is important to me. This app does not process any personal data.

2. No Collection of Personal Data

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No usage analytics are implemented
  • No advertising services are integrated
  • No third-party SDKs are used
  • No server connections are established
  • No cloud services are used

The app works completely offline.

3. Local Storage of Game Data

The app stores game progress and settings locally on your device using Unity’s “PlayerPrefs” functionality.

  • Data remain solely on your device
  • Are not transmitted
  • Are not accessible to the developer

They cannot be linked to an identifiable person.

4. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

5. Your Rights

As no personal data is processed, there are no rights of access, rectification, or deletion arising from the use of this app. You nevertheless have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If the functionality of the app changes in the future (e.g., by adding online services or advertising), this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
